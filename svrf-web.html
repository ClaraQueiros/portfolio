Svrf Website

Go to svrf.com

Svrf makes 3D searchable, shareable, and accessible on all platforms. We focus on bringing 3D into the daily lives of our users in ways that allow them to create, capture, and share 3D everyday!

ROLE

UX/UI Design

TEAM

Svrf

The Challenge

The challenge was that when Svrf launched their website in early 2017, it focused on providing users with a search platform for virtual reality content, specifically 360 videos and photos. Today we’re taking a new approach to our website that focuses on our latest product launch, a filter camera app for iOS.

My Role

I helped to define and maintain Svrf’s design system and designed the new website. Design responsabilities included research, information architecture, user flows, visual and prototyping.

The Process

The solution was to create a new website much simpler that represents the evolved brand. The new website focuses on crafting a narrative that allows users and brands alike to understand the Svrf story.
