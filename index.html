About me

I’m Clara, a Product Designer from Portugal, and currently making sleep fun and simple at Revery. Our app, Pocket Kado, is a love letter to our users by delivering cognitive behavioural therapy for insomnia (CBT-I) in the form of a game. Our mission is to make CBT-I accessible, affordable and engaging – all in one fun and interactive experience!

I'm passionate about creating experiences that make a positive impact on the world through design, and thrive in collaborative team environments.

In the past, I worked as a freelance designer where I created experiences for Coffee'n Clothes, and other wonderful companies and people.

Prior to that, I was the sole designer at Svrf - the first search API for 3D face filters - where I led design from conception to implementation, working with stakeholders to create user-centered solutions that are both functional and aesthetically appealing. In July of 2020, Svrf was acquired.

Before Svrf, I collaborated with We Are Interactive in designing the interactive pieces for the museum Museu da Vila, in Cascais after being a design intern at the Stockholm-based company Turnpike Group.

In my previous life, I worked as a nutritionist and in customer service. Along the way, I discovered design and fell in love with it.

In my logged-off hours, you can find me enjoying a good cup of coffee and a slice of pizza, skating, and traveling.

Looking to chat? Drop me a note anaclaravqueiros@gmail.com!
