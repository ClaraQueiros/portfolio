Clara Queirós

Designer

Hello!

I am Clara Queirós, a designer based in Lisbon, working remotely.

My path as a designer started as an intern at a Stockholm based company, Turnpike Group. I worked on all aspects of the products, from the interface design to the marketing content.

After my internship, I collaborated with a Portuguese agency, We Are Interactive, in the development of the UI for some interactive pieces to display in a Portuguese museum, in Cascais.

I got interested in remote work, and that's when I found Svrf (acquired by Poplar). For eight months, as a sole designer, I led design from conception to implementation, working with stakeholders to create user-centred solutions that are both functional and aesthetically appealing.

Currently I’m looking for new opportunities.

Occasionally I like to travel the world and work from different inspiring places..
